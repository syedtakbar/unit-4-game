Crystals Collector Game!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amounts of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if you total goes above the random number. The value of crystal hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
